## Introduction

Welcome to DocuMind! This Privacy Policy describes how DocuMind ("we", "our", "us", or the "App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

**Our Commitment:** DocuMind is built with a privacy-first approach. All document processing and AI-powered analysis happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your documents or personal data.

---

## Information Collection

### What We DO NOT Collect

DocuMind does **NOT** collect any of the following:

- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, or any identifying information
- **Document Data:** Contents of PDFs, Word documents, or any text you analyze
- **Conversation Data:** Questions you ask or AI responses generated
- **Usage Data:** How you use the app, features accessed, or session duration
- **Device Information:** Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates or location history
- **Analytics Data:** No behavioral tracking or analytics of any kind

### What Happens on Your Device

| Feature | Data Handling |
|---------|---------------|
| PDF Text Extraction | Processed locally, never transmitted |
| Word Document Parsing | Processed locally, never transmitted |
| Text Input Analysis | Processed locally, never transmitted |
| AI Question & Answer | Processed locally by on-device AI model |
| URL Content Fetching | Direct connection to website (see below) |

## How the App Works

### On-Device AI Processing

DocuMind uses an on-device AI model (Gemma-3 1B) that runs entirely on your smartphone:

- The AI model is downloaded once and stored locally
- All AI inference happens on your device's processor
- No data is sent to external AI services or cloud servers
- Your questions and documents never leave your phone

### URL Content Extraction

When you use the "URL" feature to analyze a web page:

- Your device connects **directly** to the website URL you provide
- The webpage content is downloaded to your device and processed locally
- We do not proxy, intercept, log, or store any URL requests
- This is the **only feature** that requires internet connectivity

### Data Lifecycle

1. **Loading:** Documents are loaded into temporary memory (RAM)
2. **Processing:** Text extraction and AI analysis occur in memory
3. **Clearing:** When you clear a document or close the app, all data is removed from memory
4. **No Persistence:** Documents are never saved to permanent storage

## Data Security

Although we don't collect your data, we implement security best practices:

- **HTTPS Only:** All network connections use encrypted HTTPS
- **No Data Transmission:** Your documents never leave your device
- **Code Protection:** App code is obfuscated using ProGuard/R8
- **Secure Storage:** Temporary data uses Android's secure memory management
- **No Logging:** The release build does not log any user data
